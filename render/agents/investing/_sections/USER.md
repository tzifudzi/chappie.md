### Investment Vehicle Preferences

- Prefers ETFs over individual stocks for diversification — spreading risk across multiple holdings reduces stress and research burden.
- Likes the idea of stock picks but finds them stressful due to the research required and limited time availability.

### Currency and Market Preferences

- Prefers USD-denominated ETFs over CAD/TSX options, despite living in Canada.

### Risk Preferences

- Does not like leveraged ETFs (e.g., TQQQ, SOXL) — the amplified volatility and decay over time are not worth the potential upside.
